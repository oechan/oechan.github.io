Lungshan Temple, Taiwan

Learn about the most well known temple in Taiwan, the Mengjia Longshan Temple which was built in 1738 by settlers from Fujian as a gathering place for Chinese settlers. Located in the old village part of Taipei, Wanhua District, this temple has stood the test of time and lasted through several natural disasters and wars.
