Introduction

Potato World

Potato World is a company that aims to provide a variety of potato products flavored according to countries and their cuisines all around the world.

Target audience

The target audience are those who love potatoes, travelling, and being adventurous. Mostly targeting the younger generation, the feel and style of the company is going to be vibrant, fun, and welcoming.

The primary goal is to experiment with global cuisines centered around potato products such as fries, mashed potato, and chips just to name a few. Playing with different flavors and showcasing them to our diverse customers is what we love to do. We hope to expand the possibilites in the concoctions that we make.
