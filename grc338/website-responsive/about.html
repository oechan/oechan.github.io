Our Story

Bitter and sweet was founded in 2011 with the mission of serving the best coffee and desserts in the South Bay community. We wanted to create create a coffee shop with an edgy, yet welcoming environment where you can work, meet with people, and just hang out with a warm or hot drink.

Our team of talented baristas will serve you expertly prepared espresso and coffee drinks, using fresh beans. We also offer handcrafted gelatos from Humphry Slocombe, with unique flavors that range from Secret Breakfast to Mint lavendar. For something more delicate, we also have pastries baked right in our kitchen.

Our goal is to provide a wonderful experience for all our guests and want you to feel at home!
